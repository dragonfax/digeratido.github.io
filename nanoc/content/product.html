Collective giving made easy and compelling

DigeratiDo is creating a mobile tool for technology company employees to donate resources and volunteer time to non-profit organizations

DigeratiDo is a platform for social philanthropy that leverages technology and social networks so that companies and individuals can immediately raise money and awareness for positive change. Our customers are individuals and corporations who are motivated to give back to their community in a way that is fun, immediate and more relevant than ever before. DigeratiDo's platform is built to connect individuals in existing social networks so that users can seamlessly donate their skills or resources to accredited local and worldwide causes while creating meaningful lasting connections. Companies using DigeratiDo can foster employee volunteerism and donate resources to promote goodwill in the communities that they serve.
